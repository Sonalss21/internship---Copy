Our History

AromaticaParadise perfume brand was established in 1985 by a group of passionate perfumers who wanted to create fragrances that would transport people to a paradise of aromas. They started by using only natural ingredients sourced from around the world, but soon realized that synthetic ingredients could also enhance their creations. Over the years, AromaticaParadise has become known for its unique blends of scents and its commitment to sustainability. Today, the brand continues to innovate and push the boundaries of what is possible in the world of perfumery.
